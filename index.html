About DCMUN

Where dialogue becomes action.

DCMUN, Dialogue for Change Model United Nations, creates a serious yet accessible platform for students to practice diplomacy, research international issues, and speak with purpose.

The 2026 online edition centers on the United Nations Human Rights Council, giving every delegate space to understand procedure, build confidence, and contribute to a committee shaped by real global questions.
